Platform Operations

Admin-only pages for capacity, reservations, availability, and observability, to support platform operations and planning.

Open Platform

What to expect

  • Capacity inventory, reservations, and availability by resource class.
  • Hybrid placement, worker-pool warnings, and active demand signals.
  • Observability and cost signals are intended for operators, not end-user workflows.

When you need more resources

  • Use the platform overview to show sustained CPU/memory pressure and reserved-capacity blocks.
  • Call out changes in active demand, protected demo windows, and pool headroom.
  • Use reservation and pool data to move heavy labs or protect curated demo windows.
